About Us

Tesla Motors is a company manufacturing electric cars and their components, founded by a pioneer in this field (as well as a pioneer in the field of space tourism and space commerce) Elon Musk. Among the company's investors are Larry Page and Sergey Brin, owners of Google Corporation. The main objective of the company is to make electric cars accessible to users of the middle price segment; reduce dependence on gasoline engines, and therefore exhaust gases. The task of Elon Musk is to prove that you can ride on electricity. Among the latest statements by Mask is Tesla, priced at $ 20,000 for a couple of years.
